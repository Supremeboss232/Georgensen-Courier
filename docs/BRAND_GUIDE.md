## BRAND OVERVIEW

**Company Name:** Georjensen  
**Tagline:** "Your Package. Our Priority. Anywhere, Everywhere."  
**Industry:** Courier & Logistics  
**Founded:** 2026  
**Headquarters:** United States (Global Ready)

### What is Georjensen?

Georjensen is an **asset-light online courier platform** that aggregates local riders and international partners to provide seamless delivery solutions. We serve SMEs, e-commerce sellers, freelancers, and corporates who need reliable courier services without managing multiple accounts.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Local convenience + global reach
- Transparent pricing & real-time tracking
- Vetted, reliable partners
- Professional, accountable service
- One interface for multiple services

## TARGET AUDIENCE

## LOGO GUIDELINES

### Primary Logo

**Format:** Wordmark + icon combination  
**Style:** Modern, geometric  
**Composition:** "Geo" icon (globe/location pin concept) + "Jensen" wordmark

### Logo Usage Rules

#### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 40px height (minimum)
- **Print:** 1 inch height (minimum)

#### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space of at least 0.25x logo height on all sides
- No elements should crowd the logo

#### Color Modes
- **Primary:** Blue on white
- **Inverse:** White on dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome:** Black/white (when color unavailable)
- **Do NOT:** Apply gradients, shadows, or effects not specified

#### Do NOT
- ✗ Rotate the logo
- ✗ Stretch or skew the logo
- ✗ Add effects (shadows, glows, 3D)
- ✗ Change colors without approval
- ✗ Rearrange logo elements

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Font Family

**Primary Font:** Segoe UI or System Default  
- Clean, modern, highly legible
- Excellent screen rendering
- Neutral, professional appearance

**Fallback Stack:**
```
font-family: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif;
```

### Font Sizes & Weights

#### Headings
- **H1:** 48px, Bold (700)
- **H2:** 36px, Bold (700)
- **H3:** 28px, Bold (700)
- **H4:** 20px, Bold (700)

#### Body Text
- **Large Body:** 18px, Regular (400)
- **Standard Body:** 16px, Regular (400)
- **Small Body:** 14px, Regular (400)

#### Labels & Captions
- **Label:** 14px, Semibold (600)
- **Caption:** 12px, Regular (400)

### Usage Guidelines

- **Headers:** Bold weight for emphasis
- **Body:** Regular weight for readability
- **Interactive Elements:** Semibold for clarity
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for body text (readability)

## IMAGERY STANDARDS

### Photography Style

**Characteristics:**
- Professional, high-quality
- Authentic (real people, real scenarios)
- Inclusive & diverse representation
- Modern, bright lighting
- Relevant to courier/logistics

### Image Categories

#### Hero Images
- Large, impactful
- Show service in action
- Human element included when possible
- Examples: Delivery in progress, happy customer, package transit

#### Supporting Images
- Medium size
- Show specific services or benefits
- Can be lifestyle or product-focused
- Examples: Local delivery, tracking dashboard, packaging

#### Icons
- Simple, recognizable
- Consistent stroke weight
- Aligned to brand color palette
- Used for status, category, or action indicators

### Image Treatment
- **Consistent Cropping:** Portrait-oriented images for some, landscape for others (varied)
- **Corners:** Slight border radius (8px) for softness
- **Filters:** Subtle, not overdone. Maintain natural colors.
- **Overlays:** Use semi-transparent blue overlay for text readability on photos

## DO'S & DON'TS

### DO
✅ Use brand colors consistently  
✅ Maintain clear space around logo  
✅ Use professional imagery  
✅ Keep design clean and organized  
✅ Ensure accessibility (color contrast)  
✅ Communicate clearly and confidently  
✅ Stay true to brand values  
✅ Test designs across devices  

### DON'T
❌ Mix fonts without purpose  
❌ Use low-quality images  
❌ Overcomplicate designs  
❌ Change colors without approval  
❌ Use sarcasm or negativity in copy  
❌ Create misleading messaging  
❌ Use outdated or trendy elements  
❌ Assume accessibility features aren't needed
