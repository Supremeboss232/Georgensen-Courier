## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Company Overview
**Georjensen** is an asset-light online courier platform that aggregates local riders and international logistics partners to provide seamless, transparent delivery solutions for small businesses, e-commerce sellers, freelancers, and corporate clients.

### Problem
Small businesses struggle with:
- Managing multiple courier accounts (complexity)
- Inconsistent delivery quality (reliability)
- Lack of real-time tracking (visibility)
- High costs from managing separate relationships
- No unified billing or support

### Solution
One-stop courier platform featuring:
- Unified interface for local, inter-city, and international shipping
- Transparent, competitive pricing
- Real-time tracking and proof of delivery
- Vetted, reliable delivery partners
- 24/7 customer support
- Corporate-friendly dashboard and API

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM:** $200B+ global courier market
- **SAM:** $5B+ SME/e-commerce courier segment
- **SOM:** $50M+ Year 5 target (1% of SAM)

### Financial Projections (Year 1)
- **Revenue Target:** $2.5M
- **Gross Margin:** 40-45%
- **Break-even:** Month 18
- **Profitability:** Year 3

### Funding Request
$1.5M seed funding for:
- Technology platform development & maintenance
- Courier partner recruitment & vetting
- Marketing & customer acquisition
- Operations team hiring
- 24-month runway

## COMPANY DESCRIPTION

### Mission
To provide small businesses and individuals with enterprise-grade courier solutions through a transparent, user-friendly platform that simplifies last-mile delivery without requiring multiple courier accounts.

### Vision
To become the trusted global logistics platform where any business can ship anywhere with confidence, transparency, and simplicity.

### Core Values
1. **Reliability:** Packages arrive safely and on time
2. **Transparency:** Customers always know package status
3. **Efficiency:** Fast, seamless service
4. **Scalability:** Local to global, no service degradation
5. **Accountability:** We take responsibility

### Service Offerings

#### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Local & Inter-City
- **Local Courier:** Same-city, same-day or next-day delivery
- **Inter-City Courier:** 1-2 business day delivery between cities
- **Target:** SMEs, e-commerce, restaurants, retail

#### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): International
- **International Courier:** Global shipping via DHL, FedEx, UPS
- **White-Label Option:** Reseller program for agencies
- **Target:** Growing e-commerce, B2B exporters

#### Phase 3 (Year 2+): Enterprise
- **API Integration:** Direct integration with customers' systems
- **Custom Solutions:** Tailored logistics for high-volume clients
- **Subscription Plans:** Monthly packages for predictable shipping

## BUSINESS MODEL

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. Shipping Commissions (Primary - 70%)
- Take percentage of shipping fees (10-15%)
- Charged to customer (not partner)
- Incentivizes volume growth

**Example:**
- Local delivery: Customer pays $10, Georjensen keeps $1.50
- Inter-city: Customer pays $25, Georjensen keeps $3.75

#### 2. Premium Features (10%)
- Insurance add-on: 5% of package value
- Priority support: $10/month
- Corporate dashboard: $50-200/month

#### 3. Partner Services (10%)
- Partner training: $500/session
- Background check: $25/partner
- GPS tracking device rental: $5/month per partner

#### 4. B2B Integration (10%)
- API access: $500-2000/month based on volume
- White-label platform: $2000+/month
- Custom integrations: $2000+

### Revenue Per Customer

#### Projected Average Revenue Per Customer

**Tier 1: E-Commerce SME**
- Average order value (AOV): $20 shipping
- Annual orders: 500-1000
- Annual customer revenue: $7,500-15,000
- Gross margin: 40-45% ($3,000-6,750)

**Tier 2: Corporate Account**
- AOV: $30-50 shipping
- Annual volume: 2,000-5,000 orders
- Annual revenue: $60,000-250,000
- Gross margin: 45-50% ($27,000-125,000)

**Tier 3: Freelancer/Occasional**
- AOV: $15-20 shipping
- Annual orders: 12-50
- Annual revenue: $180-1,000
- Gross margin: 40-45% ($72-450)

### Cost Structure

#### Variable Costs (60%)
- Payment processing: 2-3%
- Partner payout: 35-40% of customer price
- Payment fraud/chargebacks: 0.5%

#### Fixed Costs (40%)
- Platform hosting: $10K-30K/month
- Support staff (24/7): $50K-75K/month
- Partner recruitment & support: $25K-50K/month
- Marketing & customer acquisition: $30K-80K/month
- Leadership & admin: $20K-40K/month
- Technology team: $60K-100K/month

## RISK ANALYSIS

### Key Risks & Mitigation

#### 1. Partner Reliability Risk
**Risk:** Courier partners don't deliver on promises.  
**Mitigation:**
- Rigorous vetting & background checks
- Performance monitoring & rating system
- Easy removal of underperformers
- Customer refund guarantee
- Alternative partner options

#### 2. Customer Acquisition Cost
**Risk:** CAC exceeds projections, reducing profitability.  
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on high-LTV segments (corporate)
- Referral incentive program
- Organic growth via partnerships
- Content marketing (low CAC)
- Strong retention (reduce churn)

#### 3. Competitive Pressure
**Risk:** Large players (DHL, FedEx) or well-funded startups enter market.  
**Mitigation:**
- Build switching costs (integrations, habit)
- Continuous product improvements
- Focus on underserved segments (SMEs)
- Build brand loyalty through service
- Scale quickly to achieve market position

#### 4. Regulatory/Liability
**Risk:** New regulations or liability claims harm business.  
**Mitigation:**
- Comprehensive insurance coverage
- Regular legal compliance audits
- Clear terms & liability limitations
- Escrow for disputed deliveries
- Monitor regulatory landscape

#### 5. Technology/Data Security
**Risk:** Platform outage or data breach.  
**Mitigation:**
- AWS redundancy & disaster recovery
- Regular security audits & pen testing
- Data encryption (in transit & rest)
- Incident response plan
- GDPR/CCPA compliance

#### 6. Market Adoption Risk
**Risk:** Target customers prefer incumbents.  
**Mitigation:**
- Free trial & onboarding support
- Early adopter incentives
- Proven results from customers
- Strong customer testimonials
- Seamless migration from competitors
