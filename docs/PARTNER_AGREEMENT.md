# Partner Agreement

**Georgensen Courier - Partner Agreement**

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

## 1. Agreement Overview

This Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Georgensen Courier Pty Ltd** ("Company") and the Partner ("Partner") for the provision of delivery services.

## 2. Services and Responsibilities

### 2.1 Partner Responsibilities
- Provide reliable and professional delivery services
- Maintain all required insurance and licenses
- Adhere to traffic laws and safety regulations
- Communicate professionally with customers
- Handle packages with care and respect
- Update tracking information in real-time
- Complete deliveries within agreed timeframes

### 2.2 Company Responsibilities
- Provide fair and transparent pricing
- Support partners with technology platform
- Handle customer complaints professionally
- Provide training and resources
- Ensure timely payment for completed deliveries
- Maintain confidentiality of partner information

## 3. Payment Terms

- Payment Method: Bank Transfer
- Payment Frequency: Weekly/Bi-weekly
- Payment Portal: Partner Dashboard at partner.georgensen.com.au
- Commission Rate: 15% - 25% based on performance tier
- Incentives: Performance bonuses for ratings above 4.8

## 4. Performance Standards

### 4.1 Service Level Agreements
- On-time delivery rate: Minimum 98%
- Customer rating: Minimum 4.5 stars
- Response time to customer inquiries: Within 2 hours
- Damaged delivery rate: Maximum 0.5%

### 4.2 Monitoring and Evaluation
- Real-time tracking monitoring
- Monthly performance reports
- Quarterly reviews with account managers
- Suspension for sustained poor performance

## 5. Insurance and Liability

- Partners must maintain comprehensive public liability insurance
- Minimum coverage: $5,000,000 AUD
- Certificate of currency required annually
- Partner liable for loss or damage caused by negligence

## 6. Confidentiality

Partners agree to maintain confidentiality of:
- Customer personal information
- Pricing structures
- Company proprietary information
- Internal policies and procedures

## 7. Termination

### 7.1 Termination by Either Party
- 30 days written notice required
- Performance issues: 14 days notice after warning

### 7.2 Immediate Termination
- Criminal activity involving the Company
- Loss of required licenses
- Multiple policy violations
- Breach of confidentiality

## 8. Dispute Resolution

- Initial resolution through Partner Support team
- Escalation to Partner Manager if unresolved in 7 days
- Mediation through independent third party if necessary
- Final resolution through Australian arbitration

## 9. General Provisions

- This agreement is governed by Australian law
- Amendment requires written consent of both parties
- Partner cannot transfer agreement without approval
- Invalid clauses do not void entire agreement
